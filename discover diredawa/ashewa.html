Ashewa Market

Ashewa Market is one of Dire Dawa’s most iconic marketplaces, known for its colorful atmosphere, traditional items, and friendly vendors. It's a melting pot of cultures, sounds, and scents — where you can find vibrant textiles, handwoven baskets, aromatic spices, and unique street foods that reflect the rich heritage of the region. Whether you're shopping for souvenirs or experiencing everyday life, Ashewa Market offers a truly authentic Ethiopian vibe.
