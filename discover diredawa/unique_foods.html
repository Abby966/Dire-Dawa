Unique Foods of Dire Dawa

Shimo

Shimo is a traditional Ethiopian porridge made from millet or sorghum flour. It’s a staple comfort food in Dire Dawa, enjoyed especially during breakfast or as a nourishing meal. It has a smooth, creamy texture and is often accompanied by spicy sauces or stews.

Location: Commonly found in local homes and markets around Dire Dawa.Famous place include "ehetemamachochu" in number wan

Dench

Dench is a popular fresh salad-like dish in Dire Dawa, loved for its vibrant flavors and refreshing taste. It’s a staple at local gatherings and a favorite street food. You can find the best Dench at famous spots like Axste Dinch in Sabiyan and Yemegnu Dench in Gnedekore, where vendors serve it with authentic spices and fresh ingredients.

Location: Available at street vendors and famous places are "axste dench" in sabiyan and "yimegnu dench" in genedkore.

Fetira

Fetira is a layered flaky bread,egg and vegetables, often enjoyed with honey, butter, or tea. It has a tender, buttery texture that melts in your mouth, making it a favorite for breakfast or snacks in Dire Dawa.

Location: Commonly served at breakfast spots and cafes throughout Dire Dawa.
