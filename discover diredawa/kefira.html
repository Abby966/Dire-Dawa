About Kefira Market

Located in the heart of Dire Dawa.Kefira is a beloved place in Dire Dawa, especially known for its fresh and cultural spices as well as a variety of traditional food items. It offers an authentic taste of the city’s heritage and is a must-visit for anyone looking to experience the flavors and traditions of the region.
