Kefira Market

About Kefira Market

Located in the heart of Dire Dawa, Kefira Market is a vast and vibrant space known especially for its abundance of vegetables, spices, and traditional seeds. Walking through its crowded paths, you're surrounded by colors, aromas, and voices that offer a truly immersive experience.

This market isn’t just a place to shop—it’s a journey through time. With every step, you feel connected to ancient Ethiopian trade traditions and community life. The authenticity and richness of the experience make Kefira one of the most essential stops for any traveler seeking to understand Dire Dawa’s cultural rhythm.
